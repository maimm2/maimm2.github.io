I am a Computational Linguistics PhD student at University of Illinois Urbana Champaign, where I work with my advisor, Professor Lane Schwartz. I am interested in working on problems that focus on language models related to Arabic morpho-syntax.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of professors at UIUC . I worked with Professor Tania Ionin. I did my MA thesis at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign where I was advised by Randall Sadler. My MA thesis was conducted by collecting Tweets and analyzing them using The Investment Model to explore how Egyptian Twitter users position themselves using English and Arabic on the platform. I graduated with a Bachelors (Hons.)in English Literature, Linguistics, and Education from Suez Canal University, Ismailia, Egypt in 2016. For more details, check my CV or send me an email.
